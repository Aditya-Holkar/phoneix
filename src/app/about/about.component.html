Welcome to Phoenix Kaffee!

About Us

Phoenix Kaffee is a labor of love that was born on July 1st, 2023, right here in Uruli Kanchan, Pune. We are more than just a café; we are a place where the vibrant spirit of the youth meets the essence of creativity.






Meet Our Owners

Shubham Chavan

Our journey begins with Shubham Chavan, a visionary entrepreneur and the driving force behind Phoenix Kaffee. Shubham is not just an entrepreneur; he is the owner of Na Design Factory, a creative hub where innovation and imagination thrive. With a keen eye for design and an unwavering passion for creating unique experiences, Shubham is the mastermind behind the cafe's captivating and thought-provoking aesthetics. Every corner of Phoenix Kaffee is a testament to his artistic prowess, ensuring that you'll be surrounded by an ambiance that's truly one-of-a-kind.

Swapnil Gaikwad

Our other co-owner, Swapnil Gaikwad, is more than just a business partner; he's a youth icon in our community. With his dynamic personality and an innate ability to connect with people, Swapnil brings an extra layer of charm to Phoenix Kaffee. He understands the pulse of our young and spirited customers and works tirelessly to make sure their expectations are not just met, but exceeded.






Our Inspiration

Phoenix Kaffee gets its name from the mythological bird, the Phoenix, which symbolizes rebirth and transformation. We believe in the power of change, rejuvenation, and rising from the ashes. We aspire to be a place where you can relax, rejuvenate, and reinvent yourself.






Our Commitment

At Phoenix Kaffee, we are committed to offering you more than just coffee and delicious food. We aim to create an environment where your creativity can soar and your spirits can be uplifted. Our menu is thoughtfully curated to tantalize your taste buds, and our space is designed to inspire your inner artist.






Join Us

We invite you to join us at Phoenix Kaffee for a cup of aromatic coffee, a delightful meal, or simply to soak in the inspiring atmosphere. Whether you're a coffee aficionado, an art lover, or just looking for a place to unwind, you'll find a warm and welcoming environment at our café.
